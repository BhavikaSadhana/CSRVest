Problem

Despite the mandatory CSR spending, many companies face challenges in identifying credible projects that align with their corporate values and compliance requirements. Simultaneously, impact startups often struggle to access these funds due to a lack of visibility and connections with corporates.


Solution

  • For Corporations: Offers a curated list of vetted impact startups, tools for tracking project progress, and metrics for impact assessment.
  • For Startups: Provides access to a network of potential corporate partners, resources for capacity building, and guidance on compliance and reporting.
